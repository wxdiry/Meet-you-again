Meet you again

by: blank

It’s kind of weird how you make me act like this, but after all it’s no longer new to me that I feel butterflies in my stomach when my eyes meets yours. But what is the reason why a quirky girl meets a nonchalant person? It’s like I found someone to adore in the worse timing, yet I still stayed when I saw you smiling.

Somewhere in the haze, an upset expression was etched on my face. And, I was frustrated throughout the cloudy afternoon. I have known that I always give my all when it comes to multiple tasks. Maybe, just a little bit wrong or in the most troublesome way. Why though? Simply because I was into foolish things lately.

Upon seeing a new figure standing before me, my eyebrows furrowed due to the familiarity. “I think I’ve met this person before” I thought myself as I continue to stare at him. As his soft gaze meets mine, I look away shyly as a shiver runs down my spine. As my friend’s voice reverberates through the noisy place, we started to play like our childhood days. As the sun slowly sets over the horizon, I was so certain that we wouldn’t see each other again.

“How foolish I am?” I asked myself as I saw him in front of me. My mind paces back and forth like a ghost as I was staggered by his sudden appearance. At that time I feel the air leave my lungs, as my words were barely comprehensible. My voice came out as a whisper while I was telling a story to my friend. As you lean your head forward to listen to us, I look into your eyes and I saw an utopia.

The stars are now displayed on the dark night sky, along with the gentle yet cold breeze. With my lips curve into a smile, I looked at my best friend as I continue to stare at her for awhile. But, as if someone can read my mind I can suddenly feel your presence in front of me. I turn my head to glance at you, and even though your back is facing me it surprised me when I can still recognize you. It’s quite mysterious that you can make me react like this, it feels like I’m in an infinite void of bliss.

I frantically walked around as I tried to think of a solution for this irksome project. Yet, I immediately turned to look at my friend due to his shocking suggestion. “Him? For my project? That isn’t a good idea.” but the next day, as if my body moved on it’s own it was quite unexpected that I will go far for this task. The color drained from my face as I stare at your declination. I have expected that this would happen, however I can’t deny that I was saddened.

As the months past by, I have completely forgotten about you. My day began like any other day as I go on about my daily life. I ignore the infuriating noises that has been simmering for weeks. I know that Life is cruel and it’s like the flashing lights that will blind you. As the day ends, I drag myself to bed, as I let it all go straight to my head.

No one has ever made an appearance in my dreams before. So, It was quite laughable when you presented yourself in one while I sleep solemnly throughout the desolate night. It was only a peaceful game of hide-and-seek, I was the seeker, while the others went into hiding. As I look around the place, my eyes caught yours and it felt like the whole world took a paused as I continue to stare at your figure from the distance. I can’t let go of the sight in front of me, yet reality held onto me tightly

As the golden sunlight shines upon my skin, the astonished look is evident on my face. We have only seen each other thrice, therefore it caused such a strike. A great number of questions raced inside my head. Sadly, it remained unanswered up to this day. But it felt so good, as I’ve never felt so alive and happy.

As I gaze at my phone, I was amazed by the sudden information I have. A few years back, we were in the same class. Our class picture along with our Teachers, it’s a bit confusing on how I have a few memory of you. Yet, you can’t seem to remember me. I shook the sadness away, thus even though you don’t have a single memory of me I can’t help but to let a smile appear on my face

My drowsy eyes scans the unknown place in front of me as it caught me of guard due to the large number of people standing before me. Thus, in the midst of the crowds I saw your figure walking with a quick pace. My heart starts beats like footsteps on the ground. For the past few months that I have not seen you, this is such an unfortunate way to get a glimpse of you. Yet, I walked past you with a blush on my cheeks, that was the closest I can get to you

I was suddenly taken aback by the action I did. A keychain in my hand that I bought and a strong feeling for you, along with the rush of confidence, all of it felt new. This chance only comes once in a lifetime. Yet, doubt and shyness got the best of me. Perhaps that is the reason I have failed to approach you in person.

As the night sets upon the city, I was hesitant to send you a message. Thus, I gathered all the courage I have as I knew that there would be no damage. To have a conversation with you was truly whimsical. I am completely aware that I would be a mess the second I realized my incautious actions. Yet, I’m speechless to the point that I didn’t even noticed how confident I am when I had a talked with you.

The small interactions we had was enough to keep me up all night. It made me want to dance all by myself. It was enchanting that I finally saw you again after all these years and months. From the distance, I could see it all begin. This was such a breathtaking way to meet you again.
